IronSight

Ironsight Capital, LLC was founded in 2008 as a Private Equity firm providing debt and equity financing and consulting services to small businesses to help them grow and reach their maximum potential. Ironsight focuses primarily on early stage small capitalization businesses that are cash-flow-positive, and serving the manufacturing, financial services, distribution, building products, construction, and life science industries. In addition to making capital investments, Ironsight works with business owners and management teams to develop and refine growth strategies and best practices to help businesses achieve significant and sustainable growth.
